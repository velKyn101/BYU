Our Story

Our love story is a testament to the power of destiny, how it orchestrates meetings, creates connections, and leads two souls on an incredible journey. It all began with a twist of fate and a common friend's attempt to introduce us. At the time, I was hesitant, unsure if I should take that leap into the unknown. I wasn't eager to meet someone new, but little did I know that destiny had other plans for us.

One day, as if guided by an invisible force, we found ourselves on Instagram. Our conversation started naturally, effortlessly, as if the universe conspired to bring us together. We began chatting, sharing stories, and finding common interests that bound us closer with each passing day. It was as if we'd known each other for a lifetime, and the conversations flowed easily and fast.

As we got to know each other, the undeniable chemistry between us became more and more apparent. It was as if our hearts recognized each other from across the distance that separated us. Even when work took me to São Paulo, miles away from Teresina, we found a way to stay connected. Our relationship blossomed as we navigated the challenges of a long-distance love, proving that when love is real, it can conquer any distance.

After enduring five long months of suffering for the separation, I made a life-altering decision. I decided to take the leap and follow my heart back to Teresina, the city where she lived. I left behind the bustling streets of São Paulo, the place I once called home, in pursuit of a future together.

Our love story transcends geographic boundaries. I hail from the southern regions of Brazil, while she calls the northeast her home. It's a love story that defies distance, cultural differences, and time zones. Our love is a beautiful testament to how destiny works in mysterious ways, bringing two people together from opposite ends of a vast and diverse country.

It's a story that reminds us that when two hearts are meant to be together, the universe aligns in their favor. As we embark on this journey to marriage, we are filled with gratitude for the twists and turns, the chance encounters, and the beautiful path that destiny has paved for us. We invite you to be a part of our love story as we celebrate the work of destiny that brought us together and continues to shape our shared future.
